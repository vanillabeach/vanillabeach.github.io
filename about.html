About John-Paul Holt

I am a web and mobile developer, and UX Engineer based in London, UK.

I specialise in great front-end architecture, from collaborating with designers and brand engineers, to figuring out the best technical solutions for building the user interface. This might involve building a prototype, or iterating on existing code, or even reacting to unexpected changes and determining the best way forward.

I come from a strong coding background and have a passion for building websites in my spare time. I enjoy experimenting with new frameworks and emerging technologies such as WebGL and Canvas. Experience has taught me about the power of innovation when it comes to solving problems, and that custom solutions often go hand-in-hand with third party libraries.

I also like creating artwork and enjoy drawing and photography in my spare time. I find myself using visual tools to help solve problems, whether it s using Photoshop, Figma, or just sketching on a post-it note. This puts me in a unique position whereby I feel comfortable working with both Software Engineers and UX Designers, especially when we look of improving the collaboration between the teams through better processes and understanding.
